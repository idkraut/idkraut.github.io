Hello! I'm a software engineer with years of experience in Ruby, Javascript, and Go, as well as a variety of frameworks.

Most recently, I built Hypha, an observability solution for distributed systems.

  • Explore

Hypha is an open-source observability framework for distributed systems that combines the context of traces with the detail of logs. It deploys an end-to-end telemetry pipeline that sets up distributed tracing, aggregates existing logs, and correlates logs with traces.

Hypha’s UI facilitates a debugging workflow that minimizes context switching, allowing users to flow between logs and traces to get a full view of their system.
